Voted #1 Music Academy by Los Angeles families, Prado Music Academy offers the finest private music lessons in the Los Angeles area. Our specialties include piano, acoustic or electric guitar, ukulele, drums, voice/singing, violin, clarinet, cello, and software and recording lessons. Our music academy intends to deliver the highest levels of excellence in music education to students of all ages and levels and brings together a community of dedicated, experienced, world-class educational team, hand-picked for their enthusiasm and patience. Our teaching methods are individualized, comprehensive, and fun! They are completely oriented to developing the musical abilities of the students with a focus on building a strong music base. It teaches them to discover and explore music while incorporating a playful approach to ear training, music theory, intonation, and rhythm, and emphasizing the acquisition of skills and techniques necessary to reach the freedom of artistic expression. Join us to experience how fun music lessons can be at the Prado Music Academy location in Granada Hills, Calabasas, or Online from the comfort of your home!

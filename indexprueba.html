LESSONS

30 MINUTES

30'

Perfect way to get started with learning a new instrument. Thirty minutes is enough time to cover the basics, learn a new song or technique, and get feedback on your progress. They are also easier to fit into a busy schedule.

More info.

45 MINUTES

45'

A great way to get an in-depth instruction and more personalized feedback from your instructor. It gives you more time to practice during the lesson, which will improve your skills quicker. Perfect for students who are looking to take their playing to the next level.

More info.

60 MINUTES

60'

For students who want to make significant progress in their playing, 60 minute music lessons are a must. With a full hour of instruction time, you can cover a lot of ground during each lesson, from advanced techniques to more complex musical concepts. Longer lessons also allow you to spend more time on areas that you struggle with.
